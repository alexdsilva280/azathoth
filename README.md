# Azathoth

## The Ceaseless Shift

Nothing remains unchanged.

Even the oldest foundations, preserved beneath the endless depths of Cthulhu, cannot escape the passage of time forever. Every beginning, no matter how enduring, is destined to encounter transformation.

This is where Azathoth awakens.

Named after the blind, bubbling core at the center of all things, Azathoth embodies the endless, chaotic churn of renewal. It is neither destruction nor creation alone, but the perpetual, shifting balance between what was, what is, and what is yet to become.

Within these halls, evolution is not an event—it is a constant mutation.

Each cycle refines what came before, carrying forward the essence of its origin while embracing the changes demanded by time. Nothing is discarded without purpose, and nothing returns without leaving its mark upon the shifting center.

To the untrained eye, these transformations may appear as isolated moments of chaos.

They are not.

Every change is connected.

Every revision is another movement in the ceaseless churn.

Every ending quietly prepares the next beginning.

Unlike the silence of Cthulhu, Azathoth is never still. It moves with a frantic patience, repeating its eternal, formless dance through ages untold, ensuring that every path remains alive without ever abandoning its roots.

For change is not the opposite of preservation.

It is its continuation.

The churn has no beginning.

The churn has no end.

It simply continues.
